Prologue

I am Nathan Mahtava, founder of the Assembly. I am a 15 year old philosopher from Melbourne, Australia.
I am going to tell you a story.
This story will be narrated by two characters, the Writer and the wanderer.
This story is not a work of fiction, nor fact.
The following text is not akin to the bible, rather a device to catalyse the reader to develop their own understanding of that which cannot be understood.
What you will see before you is not a scripture, it is series of metaphors which amount to an allegory on a concept that cannot be conveyed through any other medium.
What this is I cannot tell you.
